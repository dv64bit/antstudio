Personal Cooling

A new approach through personnel cooling. Air conditioning the whole room consumes a lot of energy, how about we create a cool bubble around us instead of cooling the whole space? The Ether is a portable unit which effectively cools the micro space around you instead of the whole room, thus reducing the energy consumption as a whole.

Smart Hack! - With Ether next to your most favorite space like your work station or table,  You can set  your AC at 28-30degrees and enjoy the cool breeze next to the area where you use the most! With a provision for your favorite plant, Ether also humidifies the spaces which is good for skin in dry summers!

Facades

Majority of the heat gain in the buildings is through roofs and building facades. Creating an insulation barrier on these surfaces reduces the heat gain and cooling demand in our buildings. Through CoolAnt variation we are testing out innovative Hybrid Jali solutions with Air Cooler that can provide both insulation and cooling.

Integrating it architecture further
Our facade solutions can be retrofitted in an existing structure, or even integrated in an upcoming construction, in order to passively cool the building and reduce the energy requirements significantly.
